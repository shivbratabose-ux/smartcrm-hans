06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Do I need WiseHub if I already use iCAFFE?
It's already running under your iCAFFE. You don't buy it separately unless you want to extend it with your own integrations.
Can this replace MuleSoft / Boomi?
For logistics workloads, yes. For general enterprise integration beyond supply chain, we're complementary rather than replacement.
Data residency?
Cloud hosting in India, EU, US, UAE, South Africa. Dedicated deployments for regulated customers.
SDK for custom connectors?
Yes — TypeScript and Python SDKs. Our team can also build connectors for you.
Pricing?
Per-event or flat-rate tiers. Included at no extra cost with iCAFFE, WiseCargo, WiseCCS enterprise tiers.
