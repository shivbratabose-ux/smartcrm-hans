06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Is this a replacement for Navis or CTOS?
For regional ports, ICDs, and CFSs — yes. For 10M+ TEU gateway terminals, no. We don't compete with Navis at that scale.
Equipment integration?
RTG, RMG, reach stackers, straddle carriers. OEM-agnostic via standard PLC/serial/CAN interfaces.
Customs & regulatory?
Indian ICEGATE integrated. EU NCTS and UCC on roadmap for 2026.
Beta terms?
Early-access pricing through 2026. 3 ports live; 4 more in rollout.
On-premise support?
Yes. 1 of 3 beta deployments is fully on-premise (air-gapped); 2 are hybrid cloud.

Ready to see WisePort on your data?

Talk to us if you operate a regional port or ICD. We're open about what we can and can't do.
