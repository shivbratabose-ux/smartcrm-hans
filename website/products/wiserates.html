06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Can we import rates from any format?
Excel, CSV, PDF, XLSX — yes. We've processed rate sheets from 280+ carriers; new formats onboard in hours, not weeks.
Does it work with our TMS?
Yes. Native connectors for CargoWise, SAP TM, Descartes, and a generic API for custom TMS.
How are surcharges handled?
Centralized surcharge library with effective dates, lane filters, and currency conversion. Never re-key another FSC.
Can customers quote themselves?
Yes — customer portal + API gives your customers a branded quote surface. Included.
Standalone or with iCAFFE?
Both. WiseRates ships standalone for quote-heavy forwarders, or embedded inside iCAFFE for end-to-end execution.
