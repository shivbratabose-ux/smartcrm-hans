06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Who is WinAir for?
Regional carriers (sub-50 aircraft), charter/ACMI operators, new airline launches, and cargo-passenger combo operators.
Can we keep our existing GDS relationship?
Yes — we integrate with your existing agreements. No renegotiation needed.
What about IOSA/ISAGO audits?
WinAir produces the documentation trail auditors expect. Several of our operators have passed audits with us in-seat.
Combination cargo-passenger?
Yes. WinAir integrates with WiseCargo for combi and freighter operations.
Cloud or on-premise?
Cloud-native. We do not sell on-premise; regionals don't need the overhead.

Ready to see WinAir on your data?

Tell us your fleet and routes. We'll show you WinAir running your schedule.
