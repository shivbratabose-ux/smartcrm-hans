06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Does WiseHandling work as a GHA platform?
Yes — 6 of our 18 live stations are third-party GHAs handling multiple airlines under separate contracts and rate cards.
Which handheld devices are supported?
Zebra TC-series, Honeywell CK65/CT40, Android/iOS phones with Bluetooth scanners. We ship a reference hardware kit for evaluation.
What about DG and pharma compliance?
Built-in DG segregation rules by IATA class, pharma CEIV workflow, temperature zone mapping, and live cold-chain audit trail.
How does it handle peak-day volume?
Horizontally scalable — Diwali, Chinese New Year, perishable peaks tested in production. Our largest station handled 3,200 AWB acceptances in a 24-hour window.
Can we run it on-premise?
Yes. Cloud-hosted is recommended for smaller stations, on-premise available for regulatory or latency reasons.
