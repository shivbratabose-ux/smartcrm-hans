06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Do we need RFID?
No — BCBP barcode works at every checkpoint. RFID is optional and can be rolled out per-station.
Which airports are supported?
Any airport with IATA bag tag standards. We're live at 24 airports across 8 airlines today.
What if our DCS is legacy?
We have EDI BSM gateway support for legacy DCS systems. In practice, we've yet to find one we can't interface with.
Can it work in hub-and-spoke?
Yes. Transfer bag reconciliation between flights is a first-class feature, not an afterthought.
Cloud or on-premise?
Both. Airport edge deployments for low-latency reconciliation; centralized BRS in cloud.
