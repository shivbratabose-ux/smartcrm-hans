03 / The product

Neutral, not nested.

We don't own the cargo, we don't sell the freight, we don't pick a carrier favorite. WiseTrack is pure visibility — the neutral layer your whole ecosystem can trust.

DCSA-ALIGNED

The ocean data standards, done right

DCSA track-and-trace, booking, and e-BL standards implemented correctly. Your ocean carriers' DCSA investments actually pay off when you route through us.

PRIVATE DATA

Your data stays yours

We don't aggregate your shipment flows into indexes we sell back. Your data is yours. Our business is software, not brokered information.

06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

How is this different from Project44 or FourKites?
We're a neutral visibility layer focused on carriers in emerging markets (especially APAC, Middle East, Africa) where the big platforms have thin coverage. We also ship IoT hardware.
Can we white-label it?
Yes. 91% of our forwarder customers use white-labeled customer portals. Full domain + theming + logo.
Do you provide the IoT devices?
Yes — managed fleet of 18,000 active. Cellular + satellite backhaul, 3-year battery, IATA-compliant for air shipment.
What about e-BL?
DCSA-aligned eBL exchange with Maersk, MSC, Hapag-Lloyd, ONE, CMA CGM. More carriers quarterly.
Pricing model?
Per shipment tracked + IoT device fees. Volume tiers available; starts at $0.40/shipment.
