06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Is this a full CMMS replacement?
Yes for GSE. For landside fleet and buildings, we integrate with your existing CMMS.
Which GSE vendors are supported?
Vendor-agnostic. We've worked with TLD, Textron, JBT, MULAG, and custom-built units. CAN-bus and telematics standard.
When does it hit GA?
Q3 2026. Early-access customers get pricing locked for 3 years.
Multi-station networks?
Yes. Central fleet, local operations. 1 of our beta customers runs 3 stations from one WiseGSE instance.
Driver app?
iOS and Android, with offline mode. Designed for gloves, daylight, and noise.
