06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Do my customers see "Hans Infomatic" anywhere?
Only if you want them to. Default is fully white-labeled: your domain, your brand, your email sender. We can include a small "Powered by" if you prefer.
Is it included with other Hans products?
Yes — WiseCustomer is included at no extra licence cost for iCAFFE and WiseRates customers. Standalone pricing also available.
Multi-tenant branding?
Yes. Franchises, subsidiaries, or regional offices can each have their own theme in one deployment.
SSO?
SAML, OIDC, Google Workspace, and Microsoft Entra. Customer-side SSO supported too.
Can I extend with custom pages?
Yes. Page-level customization through our CMS, or fully custom React modules via our SDK.

Ready to see WiseCustomer on your data?

Send us your brand guide. We'll send back a working portal themed for you within 5 days.
