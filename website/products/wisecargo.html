06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Is WiseCargo IATA-certified?
Yes — Hans Infomatic is an IATA Strategic Partner. WiseCargo implements the full Cargo-IMP and Cargo-XML message suites, tested in production with partner carriers.
Can we run WiseCargo alongside our existing PSS/reservations?
Yes. We connect to most PSS/reservation systems via standard EDI, and provide our own cargo reservations module if you don't have one.
What about CASS-India / CASS-global?
CASS-ready out of the box. We handle CASS submissions, prorate calculations, and interline billing for all CASS regions.
How long does onboarding take?
Typical airline go-live: 12–18 weeks for a 3-station launch. We run parallel with your current system for 4–6 weeks before cutover.
Can GSAs use it independently?
Yes — we have a GSA edition with territory segmentation and sub-agent hierarchy support. 6 of our deployments are GSA-only.
