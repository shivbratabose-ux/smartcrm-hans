06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

Is WiseCCS an ICEGATE ASP?
Yes — we are a certified ICEGATE Authorised Service Provider. All filings route through a direct, signed channel with end-to-end audit trail.
Which ports are live?
14 ports across sea and air: JNPT, Chennai, Mundra, Nhava Sheva, Kolkata, Kandla, Cochin, Tuticorin; and air at DEL, BOM, MAA, BLR, CCU, HYD.
How does the AI HS classifier work?
Trained on 8 years of Indian customs rulings and tariff updates. Returns top-3 codes with confidence scores and the ruling citations behind each — you can override, but you have a paper trail.
Can we migrate from our current CHA software?
Yes — we provide migration tooling for the top 4 Indian CHA systems. Historical filings are preserved; new drafts start immediately.
What about GST refunds and e-BRC?
Integrated. We reconcile shipping bill exports with GST refund claims and e-BRC closure, surfacing mismatches before they become audit issues.
