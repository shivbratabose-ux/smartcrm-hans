03 / The product

Purpose-built, not general-purpose.

An LLM can read an AWB. It can't tell you when the shipper field is actually the consignee's forwarding agent. We can, because we've seen it 40,000 times.

TRAINING

8M logistics documents

Accumulated over 8 years, representing every major forwarder, carrier, and customs regime we serve. Confidence scores based on training distribution — we know when we don't know.

DEPLOYMENT

API, batch, or human-in-the-loop

Real-time API for high-volume. Batch for end-of-day flows. Human-in-the-loop review for high-stakes customs filings where 99% isn't good enough.

06 / FAQ

Frequently asked

What doc types are supported out of the box?
24 logistics doc types: AWBs, BLs, commercial invoices, packing lists, CoOs, BoEs, shipping bills, arrival notices, DGDs, and more.
Can we train custom fields?
Yes — few-shot learning. Provide 30-100 labeled examples and spin up a new schema from the console.
Data residency?
Cloud hosting in India, EU, or US. On-premise available for regulatory-sensitive customers.
How accurate is it really?
99.1% F1 across standard fields; confidence scored per field. Human-in-the-loop consoles available for 99.9%+ workflows.
Pricing?
Per-document, tiered by volume. Enterprise unlimited available. Includes model updates at no extra charge.
