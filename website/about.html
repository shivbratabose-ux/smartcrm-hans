03 / How we work

Principles we actually live by.

Not a corporate values page. These are the operating rules — the ones we argue about in standups.

01

Ship with operators

Every product manager works shifts at a live customer site quarterly. If you haven't used your own product under stress, you don't own it.

02

Standards first

IATA, ICEGATE, WCO — we implement the spec. No proprietary lock-in on data formats.

03

Ugly workflows deserve good software

Customs queries, manifest corrections, rate amendments — the unglamorous stuff is where we invest.

04

Small teams, deep context

Every product has a 6-to-12 person team that owns the full stack, the customer relationship, and the on-call rotation.

05

Local knowledge counts

Indian ports differ from each other. African ports differ from Indian ports. We keep local expertise in every region.

06

Multi-decade timeline

We've supported customers for 20 years. We expect to for 20 more. Architectures and roadmaps reflect that.

04 / Global offices
